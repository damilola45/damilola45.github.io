about us

Plenum Check-In Software is a web application with a low development cost. It is simple to navigate with professional welcoming design and has a powerful customer demand. We have identified and listed below the needs and solution that we will want to address in this project. Plenum Check-In Software would provide companies and organizations with a new web application that is easy to navigate and provide check-in details such has time and geographical location of staff. Plenum Check-In Software would also indicate companies and organizations member staff that are absent from work and also staff that came late to work. The application will help the management to know the number of guest or visitors that comes to the company and reveal motives. The software will also convey to the company’s customers that the company is a professional and reliable company.
